Project Description: 
Do you love your roommates, but there are rifts between you? Do you hate your roommates and never want to engage in dialogue with them again? They forgot to take out the trash? They didn’t pay you back for soap? No worries, Homily got you! Homily is a roommate management app designed to reduce household tension through transparent communication and shared accountability. At its core, Homily lets users log and track household contributions, from utility payments to shared expenses like soap or groceries, so everyone stays on the same page about who owes what. The app also features a chore chart system that makes it easy to assign, schedule, and verify household responsibilities, removing ambiguity and the need for repeated reminders. We find this project to be interesting because it is applicable to everyone’s life and can be scaled from just UCLA to all college campuses and anyone who has a roommate.
